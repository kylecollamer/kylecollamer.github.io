2024 Sahlen's Six Hours of The Glen

The 2024 Sahlen's Six Hours of The Glen took place on June 23, 2024, at Watkins Glen International in Dix, New York. Serving as the sixth round of the 2024 IMSA SportsCar Championship and the third round of the Michelin Endurance Cup, the event featured top-tier sports car competition over a six-hour endurance format.
