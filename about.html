Religious Emblems Overview

The Ad Altare Dei and Pope Pius XII emblems are official religious awards of the Catholic Church. These programs help Scouts grow spiritually and explore their faith in a structured, supportive environment.

Class Location

All classes are held at Mother of Sorrows Church in Murrysville, PA.
